Statement

As an artist, I often find myself on an ever-expanding road of self-discovery and exploration. The work that I've done encapsulates the rollercoaster of experiences that have helped shaped me into the person I am today. With every drawing, I strive to create a dig into the depths of my soul, inviting the thoughts and stories to bubble up from within.

For me, I find great value in the human experience and how that coresponds with that of the natural world. I find that I'm able to express these feelings through various media by which I engage with and derive lessons from. I find art to be a transitive and meditative experience by which to explore these ideas.
