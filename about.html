About Me

I'm an undergraduate student in the Information Techonolgy program at the University of Washington. My current career goal is to work as a professional front end web devloper while traveling abroad; something that's known as a "digital nomad". I'm currently learning Japanese and in my free time enjoy reading manga, watching anime, and working out. I'm also am deeply into philosophical talks!

I picked up art a while back & spent everyday for 4 hours through the free program Draw-A-Box. Afterwards, I would spend these daily 4 hours drawing the same thing over & over again, encoding the process of art into my mind as much as I could. I genuinly enjoy just creating for the sake of it as I have quite a hyper-active imagination & my mind just feels congested if I'm not creating & expressing my ideas. I hope you enjoy the art that my modest page has to offer it brings some inspiration!
